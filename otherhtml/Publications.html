ISTE-PESCE Activities

The chapter regularly conducts workshops, seminars, and conferences on various technical topics relevant to the engineering field. They also publish reports on their activities and achievements. You can find some of these reports on the PESCE website: https://www.facebook.com/pesceiste/


Faculty Publications

Several faculty members at PESCE are actively involved in research and publish their work in peer-reviewed journals and conferences. You can find a list of faculty publications on the PESCE website: https://pescemandya.org/academic_research.php It's possible that some of these publications may be related to ISTE or its initiatives, but you would need to search through the individual publications to confirm.
