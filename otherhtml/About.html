About Us

ISTE PESCE Mandya

is a chapter of the Indian Society for Technical Education, a professional organization of engineering educators and educational administrators. The chapter was established in 1989 at the PES College of Engineering, Mandya, a premier engineering institution in Karnataka. The chapter aims to advance the cause of technical education and research, and to provide opportunities for its members to participate in various activities and programs. Some of the activities include webinars, workshops, short term courses, student projects, and awards. The chapter also organizes social events for the members and their families. ISTE PESCE Mandya is one of the most active and vibrant chapters in the country, with a strong network of faculty and students. The chapter has received several accolades and recognitions from the ISTE headquarters and other bodies. The chapter strives to uphold the vision and mission of ISTE, and to contribute to the development of technical education and society.
